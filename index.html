The Passing

The Rebounding

Maybe Jokic's most underrated skill. We have been accustomed to expect that big men should be great rebounders but this is just not true. Just look at Anthony Davis's stat line sometimes. LeBron and Austin Reaves sometimes have more rebounds than him and same goes for Deandre Ayton. But Jokic is a glass cleaner and will punish the any team if he's not being boxed out properly.
